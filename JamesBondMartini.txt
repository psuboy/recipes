James Bond Martini:
1) Fill shaker with ice
2) add one ounce of Gin
3) add two ouces of vodka
4) add dry vermouth to taste
5) cut a shard of lemon peel and rub it on outside of martini glass
6) twist lemon peal shard and place in glass
7) place lid on shaker and shake for 30 seconds.
8) strain into martini glass and enjoy
